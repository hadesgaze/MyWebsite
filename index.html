About Us

Silvenia Writerhood is a writer organization founded by Kairus Silvenia in 2016, aimed at nurturing and supporting aspiring writers. The organization provides a platform for budding authors to hone their writing skills, collaborate with open-minded individuals, and receive guidance from professional writers. Through workshops, writing contests, mentorship, and events, Silvenia Writerhood encourages creativity, storytelling, and literary growth. Silvenia is not just an organization but a home, a paradise of a writer. Whether through fiction, poetry, or essays, members are empowered to craft narratives that transcend boundaries, uniting people through the universal language of storytelling.
